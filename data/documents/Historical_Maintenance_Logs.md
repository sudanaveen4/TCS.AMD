# Historical Maintenance & Incident Logs (2025-2026)

This document contains a curated log of significant past maintenance events and incidents across the High-Moisture Extrusion plant. These records provide critical context for Root Cause Analysis (RCA).

## Incident HIST-001 (M2 - Extruder)
- **Date**: 2026-05-22
- **Machine**: M2 (Coperion Twin-Screw Extruder)
- **Incident Type**: Bearing Failure
- **Symptoms**: Vibration telemetry spiked from normal (2.5 mm/s) to critical (13.1 mm/s) over a 2-minute window. Drive unit temperature reached 85°C.
- **Root Cause**: The front thrust bearing on the drive shaft failed due to fatigue and a partially blocked oil lubrication line.
- **Resolution**: Replaced the SKF thrust bearings and flushed the lubrication lines. Downtime: 6 hours.

## Incident HIST-002 (M4 - Cutter)
- **Date**: 2026-06-07
- **Machine**: M4 (Urschel Precision Cutter)
- **Incident Type**: Jam / Motor Overload
- **Symptoms**: Current spiked to 22.0 A, RPM dropped to 0 instantly.
- **Root Cause**: An un-cooked, hard protein mass passed through the cooling die and entered the cutter, jamming the impeller.
- **Resolution**: Manually opened the cutting head, removed the dense mass, and replaced two bent blades. Downtime: 45 minutes.

## Incident HIST-003 (M3 - Cooling Tunnel)
- **Date**: 2025-11-14
- **Machine**: M3 (CryoCool Tunnel)
- **Incident Type**: Overheating / Conveyor Slip
- **Symptoms**: Throughput dropped, product exit temperature reached 90°C (normally 60°C).
- **Root Cause**: The pneumatic tensioner on the conveyor belt lost pressure, causing the belt to slip. Product remained in the tunnel too long and the cooling loop couldn't keep up.
- **Resolution**: Replaced the pneumatic seal on the tensioner cylinder.

## Incident HIST-004 (M1 - Mixer)
- **Date**: 2026-02-02
- **Machine**: M1 (Buhler Mixer)
- **Incident Type**: Vibration Anomaly
- **Symptoms**: Intermittent vibration spikes up to 6.5 mm/s.
- **Root Cause**: Accumulation of dried soy protein on the paddle shafts caused rotational imbalance.
- **Resolution**: Performed a deep clean of the drum using high-pressure steam. No mechanical parts replaced.

## Incident HIST-005 (M5 - Palletizer)
- **Date**: 2026-04-30
- **Machine**: M5 (ABB Palletizer)
- **Incident Type**: Safety Trip
- **Symptoms**: Machine completely halted.
- **Root Cause**: A worker reached into Zone Z5 to adjust a misaligned box, tripping the light curtain.
- **Resolution**: Re-trained operator on Zone Z5 safety protocols. No mechanical issues.
