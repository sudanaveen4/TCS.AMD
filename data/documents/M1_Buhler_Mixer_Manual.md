# Buhler Industrial Mixer X-2000 Operational Manual
**Manufacturer**: Buhler Group
**Machine Type**: Wet/Dry Twin-Shaft Paddle Mixer
**Location**: Zone Z1 (Raw Material Processing)

## 1. System Overview
The Buhler X-2000 is a high-capacity twin-shaft paddle mixer designed for hydrating soy and pea protein isolates prior to high-moisture extrusion. It ensures uniform moisture distribution (typically 50-60% target moisture) which is critical for consistent extrusion.

![Buhler Mixer Diagram](./images/buhler_mixer_diagram.png)

## 2. Operating Parameters
- **Normal RPM**: 1400 - 1500 RPM
- **Normal Throughput**: 450 - 550 kg/h
- **Motor Current**: 5.0 A - 15.0 A
- **Normal Vibration Limit**: < 3.5 mm/s
- **Maximum Operational Temperature (Drive Unit)**: 65°C

## 3. Maintenance & Troubleshooting

### 3.1 Bearing Failure Signatures
If the main shaft bearings begin to fail, the telemetry will show:
- **Vibration**: Spikes above 8.0 mm/s.
- **Temperature**: Gradual rise above 70°C.
- **Action**: Halt the machine immediately. Continued operation > 10 mm/s will result in catastrophic shaft misalignment. Replace the SKF front-end bearings.

### 3.2 Blockage / Jam
If dry ingredients clump heavily due to uneven water dosing:
- **Current**: Spikes above 25.0 A.
- **Throughput**: Drops to 0 kg/h.
- **Action**: Manually clear the mixing drum. Check water dosing valves for clogs.

## 4. Safety
- **Lockout/Tagout**: Ensure total power isolation before opening the drum cover.
- **PPE Requirements**: Safety glasses, steel-toe boots, and dust masks when handling dry protein isolates.
