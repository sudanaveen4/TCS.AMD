## **Root Cause Analysis:**

### Direct Causes:
- The primary cause of this incident was a failure to detect and identify potential jamming points before production started.
  
### Contributing Factors:
- Inadequate visual inspection techniques leading to missed indications of internal process issues.
- Failure to maintain continuous monitoring during operations.
- Insufficient training on how to recognize signs of jamming under different operating conditions.

## **Corrective Actions:**

**Immediate Fixes:**
1. **Training Program:** Implement a comprehensive training program for operators and maintenance staff covering all aspects of visual inspection techniques, including advanced methods like infrared scanning to detect internal issues.
2. **Continuous Monitoring System Integration:** Integrate real-time monitoring systems with the machine's control panel, ensuring that any anomalies are detected early.
3. **Reinforcement Inspection Schedule:** Establish a strict schedule for regular visual inspections and periodic re-inspections of critical components.

## **Preventive Recommendations:**
